Tap Tap Table - Smart Dining System: Low-Fidelity Storyboard and Mid-Fidelity Wireframing

Introduction

Tap Tap Table is a smart dining system that seamlessly blends modern technology with the traditional dining experience. Our interactive smart table interface empowers guests to manage their entire dining journey, from making reservations and checking in to placing orders, requesting water/additional assistance, and making payments, all with a simple table tap from their seat. While chefs and wait staff remain essential for food preparation and delivery, Tap Tap Table digitizes the rest of the experience to enhance convenience and personalization.

Guests can easily browse digital menus, place orders, and check out at their convenience, eliminating the need to wait for the check or flag down a server. To better enhance the customer experience, real-time notifications keep restaurant staff informed of orders, service requests, and bussing needs, improving overall efficiency within the restaurant. A streamlined, color-coded system alerts the wait staff of customer needs, ensuring a smoother, more responsive dining experience.
