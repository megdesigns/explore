About Me

I’m a UI/UX Designer passionate about crafting intuitive and engaging digital experiences that solve real-world problems. With a strong focus on understanding user needs, I translate insights into visually appealing and functional designs. My goal is to create products that not only look great but also deliver seamless, user-centered experiences.

Currently pursuing a MS in Human-Computer Interaction and User Experience at Drexel University, I bring a multidisciplinary perspective to design. My background in Political Science has equipped me with sharp analytical skills, which I apply to ensure my work aligns with both user needs and business goals. I thrive in collaborative environments where I can share ideas, learn from others, and grow as a designer.

I believe great design is a balance of creativity, strategy, and empathy. My design process is rooted in user-centered principles


My Design Approach

Research & Discovery;

I start by understanding the problem space, gathering insights through user research, competitive analysis, and stakeholder collaboration.

Ideation & Exploration;

I brainstorm, sketch, and prototype ideas, iterating based on feedback and testing to refine the best solutions.

Design & Execution;

I craft clean, visually appealing, and functional designs that enhance the user experience.

Testing & Iteration;

I continuously test and refine my designs, ensuring they are intuitive, accessible, and impactful.


I’m proficient in Figma and experienced in user research, usability testing, prototyping, and tools like Framer. I also have foundational experience in web development (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript). My journey into design has also led me to explore creative and technical areas like 3D modeling with Blender and building websites.


Throughout my design journey, I’ve also explored other creative and technical fields like Blender modeling and website development. I learn something new almost every day, and it’s been an exciting journey. In fact, I’m more convinced than ever that you can be whoever you want to be and achieve whatever you set your mind to if you put in the work.


Outside of design, I enjoy playing video games—especially EAFC—and traveling. While I’m not a fan of long flights or drives, I love discovering new places, experiencing different cultures, and meeting new people.

I’m always open to new challenges and opportunities to grow.
